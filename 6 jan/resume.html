Energy trading in the context of off-grid solar systems refers to the exchange of surplus solar energy between independent solar power producers. These producers, often households or businesses, generate their own electricity using solar panels and store excess energy in battery storage systems. When the stored energy exceeds their own consumption needs, they can sell this surplus energy to other consumers on the grid who need it.
